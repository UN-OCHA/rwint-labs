--- id: rw-project-mobile title: ReliefWeb Mobile v1 origin: RW Ideas image: img/rw-thumbnail-mobile.png started: January 2013 ended: June 2018 weight: 30 --- A very light and quick site showing ReliefWeb content, without any javascript and a simple, minimal design that worked on all browsers. Borrowing some improvements from ReliefWeb Lite, version 2 is still very light and quick and works on all browsers, but with an improved design, better accessibility and a little bit of javascript to allow it to work offline.
