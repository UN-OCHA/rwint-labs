--- layout: project id: humanitarian-events-service title: Humanitarian Events origin: Community image: img/rw-thumbnail-events.png started: June 2017 ended: May 2019 weight: 60 --- A dedicated, independent service allowing members of the humanitarian community, verified through Humanitarian ID, to publish information about upcoming meetings, conferences and trainings. The site was built to be compatible with Humanitarian Response and any other humanitarian site. It may still be revived, but in the meantime, given its limited usage and ongoing security and maintenance costs, the project has been taken offline.
