--- id: hum-encyclopedia title: Humanitarian Encyclopedia Dashboard link: https://humanitarianencyclopedia.org/analysis phase: Released origin: Community image: img/rw-thumbnail-hum-enc.jpg weight: 250 --- The Humanitarian Encyclopedia Dashboard is a visualization tool for studying humanitarian texts. It includes full-text content of ReliefWeb reports in English, French and Spanish (about 1 million texts and 2 billion words). Users can view trends in humanitarian discourse and conduct analyses using Sketch Engine. To test the current version, please contact he.rw.corpus@gmail.com.
