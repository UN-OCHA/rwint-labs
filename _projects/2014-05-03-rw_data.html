--- id: rw-project-data title: Humanitarian Data Exchange link: https://humdata.org phase: Released image: img/rw-thumbnail-hdx.png origin: community weight: 60 --- The Humanitarian Data Exchange (HDX) is an open platform for sharing data. The goal of HDX is to make humanitarian data easy to find and use for analysis. Launched in July 2014, HDX has been accessed by users in over 200 countries and territories.
