--- id: search-converter title: Search Converter link: https://reliefweb.github.io/search-converter/ phase: BETA origin: RW Ideas image: img/rw-thumbnail-search-converter.png weight: 170 --- Building queries for the ReliefWeb API can quickly become complex for anything beyond very simple queries and filters. A quick solution is to use the filters and advanced search on the ReliefWeb site to narrow down the content, and then copy and paste the url to the search converter which will transform it to a GET or POST request that the API will understand.
